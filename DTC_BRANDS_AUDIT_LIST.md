## LinkedIn Outreach Template

### Message 1: Sustainability Manager/Founder
```
Hi [First Name],

I came across [Brand] while researching brands leading on supply chain transparency.

Quick question: with EU Digital Product Passport requirements coming in 2027,
how is [Brand] preparing material traceability documentation?

We're offering free traceability audits to 10 sustainable fashion brands this month
- would love to share a quick scan of your supply chain visibility.

No pitch, just data. Interested?

Enzo
Founder, Fabric Passport
```

### Message 2: Follow-up (no response after 5 days)
```
Hi [First Name],

Following up on my note about the free supply chain audit.

I ran a quick scan on [Brand]'s public traceability data - found [1 specific insight,
e.g. "material origin info on 3/12 products"].

Happy to share the full (free) report if helpful.

Enzo
```

### Message 3: Operations/Supply Chain Manager
```
Hi [First Name],

Noticed [Brand] sources from [country if known] - we're building a tool to help
brands verify supplier certifications and generate EU DPP-compliant documentation.

Offering free pilot access to 10 brands. Worth a 10-min chat?

Enzo
Fabric Passport
```
