# Fabric Passport - Supplier Outreach Vietnam

## Objective
Validate the Vietnam supplier channel by contacting 3 textile manufacturers to confirm:
1. Willingness to share traceability data (material origin, certifications)
2. Interest in DPP (Digital Product Passport) integration
3. Pricing for small-batch orders (MOQ)
4. English communication capability

## Call Script (15 min)

1. **Intro (2 min):** "I'm Enzo from Fabric Passport. We help EU fashion brands
   find verified suppliers and generate Digital Product Passports."

2. **Validation questions (8 min):**
   - Do you currently work with EU brands under €1M revenue?
   - What traceability docs do you provide? (certificates, BOL, material origin)
   - Are you aware of EU DPP requirements coming 2027/2030?
   - What's your minimum order for new brands?

3. **Value prop (3 min):**
   - "We pre-verify suppliers and match them with EU brands needing compliance"
   - "You get qualified leads, brands get trusted supply chain"

4. **Close (2 min):**
   - "Can I send you a one-pager about our supplier program?"
   - "Would you be open to a pilot with 1-2 brands?"
