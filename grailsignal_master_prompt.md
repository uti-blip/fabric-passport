## 0. EXECUTIVE CONTEXT

### What is GrailSignal?
GrailSignal is a B2B SaaS platform that gives luxury brands real-time visibility into the secondary market. We answer: "What is happening to our products after they leave retail?"

### Core positioning
> **Secondary market intelligence for luxury brands.**
> Pricing, grey-market flow detection, unauthorized dealer monitoring, demand signals, and resale brand-equity dashboard.

### Why now (structural)
- Luxury resale market: $220B today → $360B by 2030 (10% CAGR, 3× primary)
- DPP EU 2027 forces traceability — brands need data infrastructure
- AI makes listing normalization and anomaly detection 10× cheaper
- Platforms (Chrono24, The RealReal, Vestiaire) reached data-mature scale
- No incumbent owns "resale intelligence OS for luxury brands"

### Wedge
Start with **watches & handbags** for **mid-tier luxury brands** (Breitling, IWC, Tudor, Celine, Loewe) who have high secondary liquidity but low internal visibility.

### Target customer
- Primary: Head of Strategy / Market Intelligence / Pricing at mid-tier watch & handbag brands
- Secondary: Brand Protection / Legal, Merchandising / Product teams
- Avoid first: Chanel, Hermès, Louis Vuitton (too closed, long sales cycles)

### Business model
SaaS B2B + intelligence reports:
- Pilot: €3,000–7,500 for 6 weeks
- Starter: €1,500/month (1 brand, 50 refs, monthly report)
- Pro: €5,000/month (250 refs, weekly alerts, dashboard, competitor benchmark)
- Enterprise: €30,000–100,000/year (multi-market, API, dedicated support, custom sources)

### Founder profile (Enzo)
- Dev fullstack: Next.js 14, FastAPI, Python, TypeScript, Supabase
- Background: economics L3 + freelance closer
- Languages: FR native, EN C2, VI bilingual, DE B1
- Location: Paris (heart of European luxury)
- Age: 21
- Mindset: bootstrap-first → VC path
- Constraints: solo founder, MVP budget <€5K, $10K MRR before seed
